Blog Maksym Istomin 6-A

Hello, my name is Maksym Istomin and I live in Mykolayiv. I like travelling very much and I want to tell you something about it. Most of all I like my trip to Turkey. There’s a very beautiful sea and excellent service in hotels. There were a lot of swimming-pools in our hotel and a football field. Every day in the evening I played football with other guys, who also lived in that hotel. I also visited Croatia, Montenegro, Greece and Egypt. When we where in Egypt, we had an excursion to Israel. I was very impressed by this trip, because I visited the birth place of Jesus Christ. I also visited the Jordan river. It was very cold. I really like the views in Croatia and Montenegro. There are very beautiful mountains and fantastic nature. In Greece I also really like our hotel, because it is one of the best in this resort. There was very tasty food. We could also rent a car for free in our hotel. There was a lot of different entertainments, such as tennis, football, chess, concerts etc. I also was in many European countries. Most of all I liked Vienna, Budapest, Munich and Krakow. These cities have very beautiful architecture. For example, the most beautiful building in Budapest is the Budapest Parliament, in Vienna – Schönbrunn. The grave of Stepan Bandera is located in Munich. What I remember most about Krakow was the trip to the Auschwitz concentration camp. I can’t say that I really liked this place, but I think that everyone should visit it. People should not forget what terrible things happened and this story mustn’t be repeated. I hope my story was interesting and useful to you.
